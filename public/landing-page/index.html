Lush Band

The Smart Bracelet for LGBTQIA+ Safety & Community

Key Features

One-Tap Emergency Alert
Double-tap the bracelet to send an emergency alert with your live location to pre-selected friends or emergency services.
Disguised as a casual tap, making it discreet for unsafe situations.
LGBTQIA+ Meet-Up Mode
Enables a “Queer Radar” feature when activated, letting you find other LGBTQIA+ people nearby (with their consent).
Perfect for networking at Pride events, parties, or even travel.
AI Mental Health Check-In
The band syncs with a mobile app that detects stress levels based on heart rate.
If stress is detected, it sends affirmations or suggests breathing exercises tailored for LGBTQIA+ mental wellness.
